About Us

Lumière de rêves

  • Who we are?

    We are Lumière de Revez, a company that transforms the essence of elegance into unique scented candles. Each creation, from charming shapes to exquisite fragrances, is designed to illuminate and beautify your home with a touch of sophistication and warmth.

  • Our Mission

    Our main mission is to craft high-quality, hand-poured candles that harness the natural power of essential oils to create relaxing, aromatic experiences. As a student-run business, we are dedicated to promoting wellness and tranquility through our products, while learning and growing together as a team.

  • Our Vision

    Our vision is to become a leading provider of artisan candles known for their ability to transform spaces into calming sanctuaries. We aspire to inspire a mindful approach to relaxation, making our candles a staple in homes, offices, and wellness spaces across our community and beyond.

  • Our Objectives

    At Lumière de Revez, our goal is to offer exceptional sensorial experiences through high-quality scented candles. We are dedicated to combining innovative design and sophisticated fragrances to create products that not only illuminate, but also beautify and transform every space into a haven of elegance and comfort. We seek to enrich the lives of our customers with details that inspire and delight.
